Syllabus

Cyber Security: Fudamental Business Risk

About this course

Taking the right security steps is essential for organizations. Because it is about risk management, protection of assets, and because practically every business relies on information to maintain a competitive advantage, Cyber security directly impacts their bottom line. This means that managers at all levels need to understand why investing in cybersecurity produces value for their companies and what issues matter today.

This course explores cyber secuirty as an critical function of business. Moreover, it explains in plain language what issues businesses should be recognizing and how how they can stay ahead of threats.

What you'll learn

• Why cyber security matters.
• How to make organizations more secure
• Privacy and security
• BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)
• Enterprise Level Mitigation
• Cyber Security in the design phase
• Persuasive technologies
• Reframe the conversation around cyber security
• How to spot vulnerabilities
• How to recovery from hacking
• Data Scientist
• How to remain one step ahead of hackers
• Encryption
• The role of personal habit in the landscape of cyber security
